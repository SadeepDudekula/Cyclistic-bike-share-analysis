==============================================
PHASE 4 - ANALYZE
==============================================
Key Findings:

FINDING 1 - Ride Frequency by Day:
- Casual riders peak on Saturday & Sunday
- Members peak on Tuesday, Wednesday & Thursday
- Conclusion: Members = commuters, Casual = leisure

FINDING 2 - Ride Frequency by Month:
- Casual riders peak in May (249,843 rides)
- Members peak in August & September (~290,000)
- Both groups ride less in winter months

FINDING 3 - Total Rides Split:
- Members: 2,642,135 rides (64%)
- Casual: 1,458,808 rides (36%)

FINDING 4 - Average Ride Duration:
- Casual riders: 19.8 minutes average
- Members: 11.8 minutes average
- Casuals ride longer per trip

FINDING 5 - Bike Type Preference:
- Both groups use classic & electric similarly
- Casual riders slightly prefer electric bikes
- Members slightly prefer classic bikes
